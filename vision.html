The Vision
Divine Touch’s vision is to connect collectors with the divine they have within, or that they connect with by external means. The motto ‘we are one, we are divine’ is not only a motto, but the truth of all nature on planet earth. Each artifact is centred around this motto, making for a cohesive theme of nature, unity, and acceptance of universal themes we face as one race.
Each collector package includes a multitude of physical and digital artifacts, all contributing to a unique and immersive 1/1 experience for every holder. Because every collector package is entirely unique, no two collectors Divine Touch experience are the same. Parallel to humanity, every 1/1 collector package is interconnected while maintaining their differences; in sound and in style, facilitating endless expansion of the Divine Touch Universe.
